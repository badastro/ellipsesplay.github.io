  • Andromeda

    Memoir before ELLIPSES

    Singularity created or birthed or adopted Andromeda. Their birth is up in the air.

    Andromeda grew up with SPT0615-JD, Cartwheel, Whirlpool, Triangulum, and Milky Way. Typically, they are the nicest Galaxy of the bunch and beloved overall. They literally see nothing outside their home. However, they want to venture onto the Fabric of Spacetime nearly as much as JD does.

    Memoir during ELLIPSES (Andromeda becomes "repulsive." They grow a white hole (opposite of a black hole).)

    Andromeda awakens. Cartwheel kindly hands over Andromeda a scope. They peek into the scope in admiration and does not pass it along to Milky Way, who is disinterested anyways. ("Bounce").

    Andromeda joins the table with Milky Way, Triangulum, Whirlpool, and Cartwheel. As usual, the other siblings bicker while Andromeda banters. Soon, Singularity gets the family to come together and say grace ("Crunch"). After Andromeda and the others say their individual graces, they realize that JD is not present. They look upon JD as they state that they basically want to run away from home ("Cutting Edge"). JD ends up getting every sibling grounded to their room ("Fixed Stars").

    The next day/millennium, Andromeda and the others are called down to get lectured by Singularity about what it means to keep their light to oneself ("Luminous"). Suddenly, JD shoots Singularity in the face. This action frees the Ellipse that housed the family and unravels our Universe. Singularity seems to have died. JD and Gravity runs away from home to the Edge of the Universe, which keeps expanding away from them. Andromeda can only watch as our Universe spreads itself out thin ("Big Bang").

    Andromeda and the remaining Galaxies lay Singularity to rest on the table. Triangulum decides that they must undo JD's mess and has Andromeda tag along. They order Cartwheel and Whirlpool to stay behind and babysit Milky Way.

    A few billion years pass, and Andromeda and Triangulum scouts out for JD. Andromeda however wanted to scope out their home at the Center of the Universe. They find that the Dome Home is vacant. Triangulum wishes that Cartwheel and Whirlpool weren't galactic idiots. Andromeda suggests that they now go find Milky Way. They turn around and head home.

    Eventually, Andromeda and Triangulum stumble upon an exhausted Milky Way. Triangulum realizes that Milky Way has been drained by the side effects of puberty, especially when they see a zit on their forehead that has been bothering them. Suddenly, the tidal dogs from back home reached up to them. Triangulum assumes that they are unfed, but Andromeda explains that they do not want the Big Bang to be reversed. Triangulum distances from Milky Way, as Andromeda decides to stay and help Milky Way. Milky Way emits intense light like a Quasar, scaring the Tidals away. Andromeda, Triangulum, and Milky Way band together to go find JD ("Fabric").

    Andromeda, Triangulum, and Milky Way save Cartwheel and Whirlpool from the tidal dogs along the way. They unexpectedly reach JD. ("Big Bounce"). Triangulum orders their siblings to reaccept JD into their lives. The other siblings are hesitant. They set up a three-dome campsite called the Continuum Checkpoint. Gravity is placed in the doghouse ellipse. Andromeda, JD, and the others speak of their adventures. JD eventually asks how Singularity is doing. Andromeda and the others lie to JD and tell them that Singularity is well and tasked them to find JD. They really assume that Singularity is gone. Triangulum goes to the command post ellipse. Andromeda remains sleeping while the others awaken to JD's defiance.

    Andromeda awakens to find the Continuum Checkpoint in chaos. For reasons not yet explained, Andromeda angrily grows a white holes and repels the incoming Tidals. Suddenly, Singularity arrives ("Big Rip"). ("It's About Spacetime").

    They return home and spend the next quillions of years scrapping by with their family.

    Eventually, JD returns home and confronts their starving but everloving family. Unlike Triangulum, they are hesitant on forgiving JD immediately... [Redacted] ("Shed Light").
